Meet the Baker.

Tiffeny C.
Owner/Executive Pastry Chef

Hello! Thank you so much for stopping by my page! It truely means the world to me. I started Sweets by Tiffeny in 2018 after decades of learning and working in the baking and hospitality industry. Currently, my cottage operation is located in Corona, California. I enjoy extra long walks with my two pups, dining out, and doing outdoor activities. My favorite items on the menu are flakey mini pumpkin pies and Biscoff cupcakes! Thank you for supporting my lifelong dream!
